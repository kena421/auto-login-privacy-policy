Privacy Policy for Auto Login

Last Updated: 17-11-2023

1. Overview

Auto Login ("us," "we," or "our") operates from the Chrome Web Store.

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our extension.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect and store cookies and local storage data in the backend for the purpose of facilitating automatic logins for users. This data is securely stored and is only fetched when other users explicitly request to log in.

3. Data Storage

The cookies and local storage data stored in our backend are encrypted and secured. We do not use this data for any purposes other than facilitating the auto-login feature.

4. Data Sharing

Auto Login does not share any user data, including cookies and local storage data, with third parties. The stored data is only accessed when other users initiate the login process.

5. Security

The security of your data is important to us. We employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect the stored data. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to safeguard your personal information, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. User Consent

By using Auto Login, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at kena421@gmail.com.
